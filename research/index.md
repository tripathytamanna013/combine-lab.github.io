# 🔬 Research

The Molecular Oncology Lab focuses on understanding the fundamental mechanisms underlying cancer development and progression. Our work integrates molecular biology, gene regulation, and signaling pathways to uncover novel therapeutic targets.

---

## 🧬 Our Research Focus

- Telomerase and cancer biology  
- Gene regulation and epigenetics  
- Signal transduction pathways  
- Tumor progression and metastasis
